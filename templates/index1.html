Get to know about H1B visa
and its selection process

The non-immigrant H1B visa helps the US companies in hiring high skilled foreign workers in variety of specialized occupations such as engineering, science and information technology. Due to the lesser annual cap value the H1B visa allocation for a fiscal year is highly competitive. Since all the applicants are highly skilled, the US selection process uses the lottery system to choose the applicants when the number of applicants is more than the annual cap value. This helps them maintain fairness among all the applicants.
